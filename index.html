Mama Imelda


The story of Imelda:

Imelda Rivera is a major character in the 2017 Disney•Pixar animated feature film, Coco. She is the former matriarch of the Rivera family who became a spirit in the Land of the Dead after her death. However, she left behind a ban of music in her family as her lasting legacy, leading her great-great-grandson Miguel Rivera to uncover the cause of her decree. She later discovers that all the information she had was warped by the evil Ernesto de la Cruz
One year later, Imelda and Héctor fully reconciled and reunited with Coco. During the Day of the dead after Miguel left, Imelda watched proudly as her great-great-grandchildren played a song.
